Privacy Policy

This is an independent third-party API gateway. We take a minimal approach to data.

What We Collect

  • Order data: Email address, selected plan, payment amount, order status. Required to deliver your API key and support refunds.
  • Usage data: Token counts per API request (cache hit, cache miss, completion tokens). Required to deduct from your API budget.
  • Payment data: Processed by PayPal. We do not see or store your PayPal password, card number, or bank details.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store your prompts or completions.
  • We do not log the content of your API requests or responses.
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize your data.
  • We do not use tracking cookies or analytics scripts.

How Long We Keep Data

Order records are kept for support and refund purposes. Usage statistics are tied to your API key and retained for the lifetime of the key. You can request deletion by emailing support@modelrelayapis.cc.

Third-Party Services

  • PayPal: Handles payment processing. See PayPal Privacy Policy.
  • DeepSeek: Upstream API provider. Your prompts are forwarded to DeepSeek for completion. We do not control their data practices.

Contact

Questions about privacy: support@modelrelayapis.cc
